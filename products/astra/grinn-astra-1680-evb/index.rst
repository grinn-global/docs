Grinn AstraEVB-1680
===================

Grinn AstraEVB-1680 is a cutting-edge evaluation board, designed for developers exploring AI and computer vision applications. Powered by the Grinn AstraSOM-1680 module, it provides a robust platform for prototyping IoT devices, smart home solutions, industrial automation, and multimedia applications.
This board stands out with its multi-camera support, advanced connectivity options, and ability to function in diverse lighting conditions, thanks to its infrared diodes and high-resolution camera interfaces. Whether you're building single-camera solutions or multi-camera systems, Grinn AstraEVB-1680 is the ideal choice for AI-enhanced vision, 3D modeling, and autonomous system development.

The user guide is available in PDF format and can be downloaded from `here <https://grinn-global.com/products/grinn-astraevb-1680/datasheet>`_.
